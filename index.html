🎯 About

Use augmented reality (AR) to pick and place a selection of furniture models into your surroundings in real-time using your mobile's live video footage. This project uses ThreeJS for rendering, WebXR for AR mode, hit testing, environment lighting, and DOM overlay for UI.

View the live demo on your mobile: https://padmavatisb.github.io/AR_RoomDecor/

🏁 Starting

  1. Open: AR_RoomDecor
  2. Click the “Enter AR” button
  3. Allow camera access
  4. Select a furniture item from the scrollable list
  5. Pan phone to find placement reticle
    6. ck the “Enter AR” button
  6. Allow camera access
  7. Select a furniture item from the scrollable list
  8. Pan phone to find placement reticle
  9. Tap screen to place model
  10. Walk around to view it in 3D
  11. Move your one finger around to translate the model
  12. Use two fingers(pinch) for scaling the model
  13. use one finger to rotate the model
  14. Tap “Stop AR” to exit
